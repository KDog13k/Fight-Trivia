About Fight Trivia

Fight Trivia is a free daily UFC and MMA trivia game. New questions drop every Monday, Wednesday, and Saturday — five questions per session covering UFC history, fighters, events, and memorable moments.

How It Works

Each session consists of five rounds with one question per round. Questions range in difficulty from Easy (50 points) to Medium (75 points) to Hard (100 points). Your total score determines your fight outcome — from Knockout Victory to Knockout Loss.

The Leaderboard

Create a free account to have your score appear on the leaderboard for each question set. Leaderboards reset every time new questions drop, giving everyone a fresh shot at the top spot.

Questions

All questions are researched and verified to ensure accuracy. If you believe a question contains an error, you can flag it directly from the results screen after completing a session.
